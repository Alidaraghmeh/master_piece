Our Vision

To enable adequate care for orphans:
  1. Until they have families.
  2. Until they don't feel fear and loneliness.
  3. Until they don't feel inferior and weak.
  4. Until they can resistance life and difficulties.

Our Mission

Protecting orphan's rights, disseminating their culture, monitoring their situation, providing legal advice and assistance to those in need, and taking the necessary administrative and legal procedures to deal with complaints of violations and abuses of orphan's rights to reduce, stop and eliminate their effects. Necessary.

Core Values

We are dependent on God, passionate about what we do, and committed to partnership.

Why We Exist

We are exist because we believe what Allah and his prophet say about the orphans and their care.

Aspirational Values

We are value relationships, humility, grace, family, the holistic care of children and orphans, and we are striving to do excellence with the ministry.
